# SLIDETHEORY — Upsell & Cross-Sell Opportunities

## Overview

Strategic upsell and cross-sell initiatives can increase Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) by 20-40% without acquiring new customers. This document outlines specific opportunities for SLIDETHEORY across the customer journey.

---

## Definitions

| Term | Definition | SLIDETHEORY Example |
|------|------------|---------------------|
| **Upsell** | Moving customer to higher-tier plan | Pro → Team, or Monthly → Annual |
| **Cross-sell** | Selling additional products/services | Templates, training, consulting |
| **Add-on** | Feature purchased à la carte | Extra storage, priority support |

---

## Upsell Opportunities

### 1. Plan Upgrade Upsells

#### Pro → Team Upgrade Triggers
| Trigger | Context | Upsell Message |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| 2nd team member invited | "You're collaborating!" | "Upgrade to Team for $99/mo and invite unlimited members" |
| Shared workspace needed | "Create your first shared folder" | "Team plans include shared workspaces" |
| Admin controls requested | "Set permissions for this deck" | "Team plans include role-based access" |

#### Monthly → Annual Upsell
| Timing | Channel | Offer |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Day 3 of trial | In-app banner | "Go annual, get 2 months free" |
| First payment | Email + modal | "Switch to annual, save $98/year" |
| 6-month anniversary | Email | "Loyalty discount: 25% off annual" |
| Churn attempt | Exit modal | "Stay annual for 30% off" |

**Expected Impact**: 30-40% of monthly users can be converted to annual, improving cash flow and reducing churn.

### 2. Usage-Based Upsells

#### Storage Upsell
```
Current: 10GB included (Pro)
Tiers:   10GB → 50GB (+$10/mo) → 100GB (+$20/mo) → Unlimited (+$50/mo)

Trigger: At 80% capacity
Message: "You're using 8GB of 10GB. Upgrade storage?"
```

#### Export Volume Upsell
```
Current: Unlimited exports (Pro)
Free tier: 5 exports/month

Upsell path: Free (5) → Pro (unlimited) → Enterprise (API access)
```

#### AI Generation Credits
```
Current: Unlimited AI (Pro)
Structure: Package-based upsell for heavy users

- Base: 100 AI generations/month
- Power: 500 AI generations/month (+$20)
- Unlimited: Unlimited generations (+$50)
```

### 3. Feature Unlock Upsells

#### In-Context Feature Gates
When user attempts to use Pro/Team feature on lower tier:

| Feature | Free User Sees | Upsell Action |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| Custom fonts | "Unlock 500+ fonts with Pro" | Upgrade modal with preview |
| Analytics | "See who viewed your deck" | Teaser dashboard with sample data |
| Advanced animations | "Pro animations available" | Side-by-side comparison |
| Remove watermark | "Export without watermark" | Before/after visual |

## Trigger-Based Automation

### Upsell Campaign Workflows

#### New User Journey
```
Day 0:  Sign up (Free tier)
Day 3:  Email - "Create your first deck" + trial offer
Day 7:  In-app - "You're 70% to your export limit"
Day 10: Email - "See what Pro users are creating"
Day 14: In-app - "Start 14-day Pro trial" (if not started)
Day 21: Email - User success stories
Day 30: Offer - "First month 50% off"
```

#### Active User Journey (Pro)
```
Week 2: Check team activity
        ↓ If inviting others → Team upgrade offer
Week 4: Storage check
        ↓ If >70% → Storage upgrade offer
Month 3: Annual conversion push
         "Switch to annual, save 17%"
Month 6: Power user identification
         → Training/certification offer
Month 12: Loyalty reward
          → Exclusive template pack
```

#### At-Risk User Journey
```
No login 7 days: "We miss you" + highlight new features
No login 14 days: "Here's what you missed" + discount offer
No login 30 days: "Pause or cancel?" (win-back)
                    ↓ Offer: 3 months at 50% off
```

## Pricing Psychology Tactics

### 1. Anchoring
Show most expensive option first:
```
Enterprise: $499/mo (anchor)
Team:       $99/mo  (target)
Pro:        $49/mo  (entry)
```

### 2. Decoy Pricing
Add a decoy to make target attractive:
```
Pro Monthly:     $59/mo (decoy)
Pro Annual:      $49/mo (equivalent - target) ✓
Pro 2-Year:      $45/mo (commitment discount)
```

### 3. The "Free" Add-on
"Upgrade to annual and get our $299 training course FREE"
