### 1. Default Annual Framing
Make annual the default selection with clear savings messaging.

**Implementation**:
- Toggle default: "Annual" selected
- Monthly shown as secondary option
- Savings prominently displayed
- "Most Popular" badge on annual

### 2. Limited-Time Launch Offers
Create urgency for early adopters.

| Timing | Offer | Target |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Pre-launch | 40% off first year (early bird) | Waitlist |
| Launch week | 30% off first year | New signups |
| First month | 25% off first year | All users |
| Quarter-end | "Save 20% before prices increase" | Price change announcement |

Reward existing monthly users for converting.

|---------|-------|--------|

| 6-month subscribers | 25% off first annual year | Day 180 |
| 12-month subscribers | 30% off + exclusive perks | Anniversary |
| At-risk (low usage) | 40% off to re-engage | Churn risk identified |

## Discount Governance

### Discount Authority Matrix

| Discount Level | Approver | Use Case |
|----------------|----------|----------|
| 0-17% (standard) | Auto-approved | Standard annual pricing |
| 18-25% | Marketing lead | Promotions, campaigns |
| 26-35% | VP Sales | Competitive deals, enterprise |
| 36-50% | CEO/Founder | Strategic accounts, saves |
| 50%+ | Exception only | Churn win-back, case studies |

### Discount Rules
1. **Never stack discounts** â€” Use highest applicable only
2. **First year only** â€” Renewals at standard 17%
3. **Time limits** â€” All promotional discounts expire
4. **Track codes** â€” Every discount must have attribution code
5. **Limit per customer** â€” Max 2 promotional annual discounts lifetime
