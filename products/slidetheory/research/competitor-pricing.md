## Detailed Pricing Breakdown

### Beautiful.ai
*Positioning: Premium AI presentation tool for professionals*

#### Plans

| Plan | Price | Billing | Key Features |
|------|-------|---------|--------------|
| **Pro** | $12/mo | Annual | Individual use, unlimited AI, 300+ layouts |
| **Pro Monthly** | $45/mo | Monthly | Same as Pro, ad-hoc projects |
| **Team** | $40-50/mo/user | Annual/Monthly | Collaboration, shared library, analytics |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Annual | SSO, SCIM, audit logs, onboarding |

#### Key Pricing Notes
- 14-day free trial (requires credit card)
- Student discount: 1 year free with .edu email
- Team pricing: $40/user annual, $50/user monthly
- Refund: 24-hour window for annual plans only
- Monthly plans non-refundable

#### Value Proposition
- **Pros:** Unlimited AI on all paid tiers, strong enterprise security
- **Cons:** No true free tier, team pricing expensive for small teams

## Pricing Strategy Insights

### Market Pricing Tiers

```
$0-7/mo:   Entry freemium (Prezi, Gamma limited)
$8-15/mo:  Standard paid (Gamma, Beautiful.ai Pro, Canva Pro)
$15-25/mo: Premium/Small teams (Chronicle Plus, Tome Team)
$25-50/mo: Business teams (Beautiful.ai Team, Pitch Business)
$50+/mo:   Enterprise (Custom pricing)
```

### Pricing Models

| Model | Examples | Pros | Cons |
|-------|----------|------|------|
| **Flat monthly** | Beautiful.ai, Canva | Simple, predictable | One-size-fits-all |
| **Per-seat** | Most tools | Scales with usage | Expensive for large teams |
| **Token-based** | Chronicle, Pitch | Pay for usage | Complexity, unpredictable |
| **Freemium** | Canva, Gamma | Low barrier | Conversion challenges |
| **Usage-limited** | SlidesAI, Prezi | Clear limits | User friction |

### SlideTheory Pricing Recommendations

Based on competitive analysis:

| Tier | Recommended Price | Rationale |
|------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Match market expectation, limit AI credits |
| **Pro** | $10-12/mo | Undercut Beautiful.ai, match Canva |
| **Team** | $15-20/mo/user | Position below Beautiful.ai Team ($40) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Follow standard enterprise practice |

#### Key Considerations
1. **Entry price:** $8-10 would undercut most competitors
2. **Team pricing:** Big opportunity - Beautiful.ai charges $40/user
3. **Value metric:** Consider slide count, AI credits, or seats
4. **Annual discount:** Standard 16-20% discount expected
