# Competitor Opportunity Gaps Analysis
## Where SlideTheory Can Win

**Research Date:** February 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After analyzing 6 competitors in the consulting slide resource space, several significant gaps emerge that SlideTheory can exploit. The market is fragmented between template providers, slide databases, and education platforms - but no one has created a cohesive ecosystem that combines all three with modern features and community.

---

## 1. WHAT COMPETITORS ARE NOT DOING

### A. Community & Collaboration
**Current State:**
- No competitor has a community feature
- Users cannot connect with each other
- No way to share customizations or get feedback
- No peer learning opportunities

**The Gap:**
- No Slack/Discord community for consultants
- No user-generated template marketplace
- No feedback mechanism on slide designs
- No "consultant network" effect

**SlideTheory Opportunity:**
- Build a community platform where consultants can:
  - Share customized versions of templates
  - Get peer feedback on presentations
  - Connect with other consultants
  - Find collaborators
  - Share industry-specific adaptations

### B. Interactive & Modern Formats
**Current State:**
- All competitors offer static PowerPoint templates
- No interactive components
- No web-based presentations
- No embeddable slides

**The Gap:**
- No Notion-style collaborative editing
- No interactive charts that update with data
- No web-based slide sharing
- No analytics on presentation views

**SlideTheory Opportunity:**
- Interactive slide builder
- Real-time collaboration
- Web-based presentation sharing
- Analytics dashboard (who viewed, time spent, etc.)
- Embeddable slides for websites/blogs

### C. AI-Assisted Consulting Methodology
**Current State:**
- SlideModel and Slidesgo have AI generation but it's generic
- No AI specifically trained on consulting methodology
- No AI that understands consulting frameworks

**The Gap:**
- AI that understands the "So what?" of consulting
- No AI slide critique ("This slide doesn't have a clear insight")
- No AI that suggests storyline improvements
- No AI that adapts to specific consulting firms' styles

**SlideTheory Opportunity:**
- AI trained specifically on top consulting firm presentations
- AI slide critique and improvement suggestions
- AI storyline generator using consulting frameworks
- AI that learns user's style and preferences

### D. Integration Ecosystem
**Current State:**
- All competitors are standalone tools
- No integrations with productivity tools
- No API access
- No workflow automation

**The Gap:**
- No Slack integration for team sharing
- No Notion embedding
- No Google Workspace/Office 365 add-ins
- No Figma integration for designers
- No CRM integration for sales presentations

**SlideTheory Opportunity:**
- Deep integrations with:
  - Slack/Teams for sharing
  - Notion for documentation
  - Figma for design handoff
  - CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot) for sales decks
  - Data sources (Excel, Google Sheets, SQL)

### E. Ongoing Education & Certification
**Current State:**
- Analyst Academy offers courses but no ongoing education
- No certification or credentialing
- No skill validation

**The Gap:**
- No "Consulting Presentation Certification"
- No ongoing skill development program
- No mastery tracking

**SlideTheory Opportunity:**
- SlideTheory Certification program
- Monthly workshops on advanced techniques
- Skill badges and credentials
- Continuing education credits

## CONCLUSION

The consulting slide resource market is ripe for disruption. Current players are either:
- **Template libraries** with no methodology (SlideModel, PresentationGO)
- **Education platforms** with no tools (Analyst Academy)
- **Slide databases** with limited customization (SlideStart)
- **Premium templates** with no community (SlideWorks)

**SlideTheory can win by combining:**
1. Methodology-driven templates
2. Real-world examples
3. AI assistance
4. Community collaboration
5. Modern workflow integration
6. Fair pricing

The opportunity is to build the **operating system for consulting presentations** - not just another template shop.
