## KEY MARKETING INSIGHTS

### What Works:

1. **Real consulting presentations as lead magnets**
   - SlideWorks' 47 McKinsey presentations page is extremely valuable
   - Analyst Academy's 600+ real presentations database
   - Creates authority and attracts targeted traffic

2. **Free tier with email capture**
   - SlideStart: 1,000 free slides
   - SlideModel: Free template gallery
   - Analyst Academy: Free email course

3. **Educational content builds authority**
   - Analyst Academy's blog covers consulting methodology
   - SlideModel's PowerPoint tutorials
   - Positions brand as expert, not just vendor

4. **SEO through massive content libraries**
   - PresentationGO: 3,373 templates
   - Slidesgo: 80,000 templates
   - Each template is an SEO entry point

### Content Gaps (Opportunities):

1. **No one has a strong newsletter** - Regular email content about consulting trends
2. **Limited video content** - YouTube presence is minimal
3. **No podcast** - Consulting methodology discussions
4. **No community/forum** - Users can't connect with each other
5. **Limited case studies** - How templates helped real businesses

### Recommended Content Playbook for SlideTheory:

1. **Create "Real Consulting Presentations" resource** (like SlideWorks)
2. **Offer free email course** on consulting presentations (like Analyst Academy)
3. **Build searchable slide database** (like SlideStart)
4. **Publish weekly blog content** on consulting methodology
5. **Create video tutorials** for YouTube
6. **Build community** (Slack/Discord for consultants)
