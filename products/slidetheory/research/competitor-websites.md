## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION INSIGHTS

### What Works:

1. **Low-friction entry**
   - PresentationGO: No registration
   - SlideWorks: Open resource pages
   - Analyst Academy: Free email course

2. **Trust signals above fold**
   - Customer counts (SlideModel, Analyst Academy)
   - Credentials (SlideWorks)
   - User testimonials (SlideStart)

3. **Clear value proposition**
   - SlideWorks: "ex-McKinsey"
   - SlideStart: "Real slides from top firms"
   - Analyst Academy: "Level up your skills"

4. **Social proof**
   - Real testimonials with names
   - Student/customer counts
   - Star ratings

### UX Gaps (Opportunities):

1. **No live chat** - Only SlideModel has "Personal Concierge"
2. **No preview mode** - Can't see templates before downloading
3. **No comparison tool** - Can't compare templates side-by-side
4. **No wishlist** - Can't save for later (except SlideStart Pro)
5. **No related templates** - Poor cross-selling
6. **No usage examples** - No case studies of templates in use

### Recommended UX for SlideTheory:

1. **Hybrid entry** - Free resources open, tools require account
2. **Template preview** - See before downloading
3. **Advanced filtering** - By industry, use case, consulting firm style
4. **Comparison feature** - Side-by-side template comparison
5. **Live chat** - Immediate support
6. **Usage gallery** - See how others use templates
