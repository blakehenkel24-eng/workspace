## PRODUCT INSIGHTS & OPPORTUNITIES

### What's Working:

1. **Real examples drive value**
   - SlideWorks includes Fortune 500 case studies
   - SlideStart has 15,000+ real slides
   - Analyst Academy offers 600+ real presentations

2. **Hybrid product models**
   - Template + slide database (SlideStart)
   - Template + education (Analyst Academy)
   - Free + premium tiers (most competitors)

3. **Beyond PowerPoint**
   - Excel models (SlideWorks)
   - AI generation (SlideModel, Slidesgo)
   - Multi-platform support (SlideModel)

### Product Gaps (Opportunities):

1. **No interactive components** - All are static templates
2. **Limited collaboration features** - No team workspaces
3. **No versioning/history** - Can't track changes
4. **No integration with tools** - No Slack, Notion, etc. integration
5. **Limited customization services** - Only SlideModel offers custom slides
6. **No community-generated content** - All professionally created
7. **No analytics** - Can't track presentation performance

### Recommended Product Mix for SlideTheory:

1. **Core Templates** - High-quality, methodology-driven templates (like SlideWorks)
2. **Slide Database** - Real example library for inspiration (like SlideStart)
3. **Educational Content** - Courses on consulting presentations (like Analyst Academy)
4. **AI Features** - Modern AI-assisted slide creation
5. **Community** - User-generated templates and feedback
