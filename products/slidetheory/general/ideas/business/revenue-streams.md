# Business Ideas

Revenue streams, growth strategies, and partnership opportunities for SlideTheory.

## Active Ideas

### Idea: Premium Subscription Tier

**Category:** business  
**Priority:** high  
**Status:** researching  
**Tags:** #revenue #pricing

**Description:**
Introduce a paid tier with unlimited slides, priority generation, and advanced templates.

**Why it matters:**
Primary revenue model. Current waitlist shows demand; need to validate price point ($29/mo vs $49/mo).

**Pricing Options:**
- Starter: Free (10 slides/month)
- Pro: $29/month (unlimited, basic templates)
- Team: $99/month (5 seats, collaboration, brand kits)

**Next Steps:**
- [ ] Survey waitlist on willingness to pay
- [ ] Build pricing page with tier comparison
- [ ] Set up Stripe integration

### Idea: Consulting Agency Partnerships

**Category:** business  
**Priority:** medium  
**Status:** new  
**Tags:** #partnership #growth #enterprise

**Description:**
Partner with boutique consulting firms for bulk licenses. Offer white-label options.

**Why it matters:**
High ACV (Annual Contract Value) customers. One 50-person firm = $3K+ MRR.

**Target Partners:**
- Tier 2 consultancies (10-100 people)
- Specialist firms (healthcare, PE, tech)
- Freelance consultant collectives

**Effort estimate:** Medium (outreach + custom onboarding)

### Idea: Template Marketplace

**Category:** business  
**Priority:** medium  
**Status:** new  
**Tags:** #revenue #marketplace #templates

**Description:**
Allow users to buy/sell custom slide templates. SlideTheory takes 20% commission.

**Why it matters:**
Additional revenue stream, community engagement, infinite template variety.

**MVP Approach:**
1. Launch with 20 premium templates (in-house created)
2. Open to contributors after reaching 1K paid users
3. Quality review process for submissions

### Idea: Affiliate Program

**Category:** business  
**Priority:** medium  
**Status:** planned  
**Tags:** #growth #partnership

**Description:**
30% recurring commission for referrals. Target: productivity YouTubers, consultant influencers.

**Why it matters:**
Low-cost acquisition channel. Consultants trust peer recommendations.

**Launch Plan:**
1. Set up affiliate tracking (Rewardful or similar)
2. Create affiliate resource kit (banners, email copy)
3. Outreach to 20 target influencers
