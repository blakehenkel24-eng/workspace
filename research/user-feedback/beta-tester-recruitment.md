# SlideTheory Beta Tester Recruitment Plan

## Overview
A systematic approach to recruiting, onboarding, and engaging beta testers to validate product-market fit and gather actionable feedback.

---

## Beta Program Objectives

| Objective | Target Metric |
|-----------|---------------|
| Product validation | 80%+ "very disappointed" in PMF survey |
| Bug discovery | <5 critical bugs at launch |
| Feature prioritization | Top 10 user requests documented |
| Case studies | 3+ publishable customer stories |
| Early advocacy | 10+ user-generated testimonials |

## Recruitment Strategy

### Target Profile Matrix

| Segment | Alpha | Beta | Open | Why |
|---------|-------|------|------|-----|
| Startup founders | 15 | 100 | 500 | Early adopters, vocal feedback |
| Marketing leaders | 10 | 100 | 500 | Scale use case, brand needs |
| Freelance consultants | 10 | 100 | 500 | Power users, diverse use cases |
| Enterprise pilots | 5 | 50 | 200 | Validation, case studies |
| Educators | 5 | 50 | 200 | Accessibility, clarity focus |
| Designers | 5 | 50 | 200 | Quality validation |

### Recruitment Channels

#### 1. Existing Network (Alpha Phase)
- **Target:** 50 users
- **Method:** Personal outreach
- **Message:**
  ```
  Subject: Early access to something I'm building

  Hi [Name],

  I've been working on a new presentation tool that uses AI to help 
  create professional decks in minutes instead of hours.

  Given your experience with [context], I'd love your feedback as 
  one of our first alpha users.

  Interested? Reply and I'll send access details.

  [Signature]
  ```

#### 2. Landing Page Waitlist (All Phases)
- **Target:** 5,000+ signups
- **Tactics:**
  - SEO-optimized landing page
  - Product Hunt "Upcoming" listing
  - Twitter/X tease content
  - LinkedIn thought leadership
  - Referral incentives (move up waitlist)

#### 3. Community Outreach (Beta Phase)
- Designer communities (Dribbble, Designer News)
- Startup communities (Indie Hackers, Hacker News)
- Marketing communities (Demand Curve, Superpath)
- Consultant networks
- Education forums

#### 4. Partner Channels (Beta Phase)
- Design agency partnerships
- Accelerator/incubator networks
- VC portfolio companies
- Consultant communities

#### 5. Paid Acquisition (Open Beta)
- LinkedIn ads (targeting by job title)
- Twitter ads (startup/tech audience)
- Google Ads (presentation tool keywords)
- Retargeting waitlist non-converters

### Scoring Rubric

| Criteria | Weight | Points |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Relevant role (target persona) | 25% | 0-10 |
| High presentation volume | 20% | 0-10 |
| Clear pain point stated | 20% | 0-10 |
| Willing to provide feedback | 20% | 0-10 |
| Influencer potential | 15% | 0-10 |

**Selection:**
- 80-100 points: Immediate invite
- 60-79 points: Standard queue
- 40-59 points: Lower priority
- <40 points: Wait for open beta

## Feedback Collection

### Continuous Channels

| Channel | Frequency | Method |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| In-app widget | Real-time | NPS + CSAT |
| Slack community | Daily | Casual feedback |
| Support tickets | As needed | Bug reports |
| Usage analytics | Continuous | Product data |

### Structured Touchpoints

| Touchpoint | When | Format |
|------------|------|--------|
| Onboarding survey | Day 1 | 5 questions |
| Week 1 check-in | Day 7 | 3 questions |
| Feature feedback | After 3 uses | In-app modal |
| Monthly deep dive | Day 30 | Full survey |
| PMF survey | Day 14, 30 | Sean Ellis |
| Exit survey | Cancellation | 3 questions |

### User Research Calls

**Schedule:**
- Alpha: Weekly 1:1s (30 min)
- Beta: Monthly group sessions (60 min)
- Open: Quarterly interviews (30 min)

**Incentives:**
- Alpha: $100/month credit + swag
- Beta: $50 gift card per call
- Open: Extended free trial + swag

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low engagement | Personal outreach, incentives, community |
| Wrong user profile | Strict screening, persona targeting |
| Negative feedback spiral | Balanced recruiting, success stories |
| Product not ready | Clear beta expectations, fast iteration |
| Support overload | Tiered support, self-service resources |
| Leaks/competitors | NDA for alpha, watermark exports |
