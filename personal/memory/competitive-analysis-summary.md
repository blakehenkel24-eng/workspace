## Executive Summary

SlideTheory is positioned well in the **value tier** ($8-15/mo) with a **balanced feature set** that avoids the complexity of Canva while offering more flexibility than Beautiful.ai's rigid smart slides.

**The $2/mo pricing mentioned in tasks appears to be outdated** - the research shows competitors at $8-15/mo entry price, and SlideTheory's ToS shows $12/mo for Pro tier.

## Pricing Analysis

### Current SlideTheory Pricing (from ToS)
- **Free:** 10 slides/month
- **Pro:** $12/month (100 slides, high-res, all templates)
- **Team:** $39/month (unlimited, custom branding, team library)

### Market Position
- **Entry price** is competitive vs Beautiful.ai ($12) and Canva ($12.99)
- **Team pricing at $39/month** is NOT competitive - Beautiful.ai is $40/user (but per-user, not flat)
- **Major opportunity:** Team pricing is confusing - is it $39 flat or per-user?

### Recommendations
1. **Clarify team pricing** - Per-user vs flat pricing unclear
2. **Consider $8-10 entry** to undercut Beautiful.ai/Canva
3. **Team sweet spot:** $15-20/user would be 50% cheaper than Beautiful.ai
4. **Add annual discount:** 16-20% is market standard

## Top 5 Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Fix Team Pricing (URGENT)

### 3. Build Embedding Capability (MEDIUM)

- Password protection
