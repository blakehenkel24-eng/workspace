## 3. COMPETITOR KEYWORD GAPS

### Major Competitors & Their Target Keywords

#### 1. Beautiful.ai
**Ranking for:** "smart presentation software," "automatic slide design," "AI slides"
**Gaps to exploit:** 
- Lacks consulting-specific positioning
- No MBB framework integration
- Generic business audience vs. consulting focus

**Keywords they rank for that we should target:**
- AI presentation maker (Position 3-5)
- automatic slide creator (Position 2-4)
- presentation design software (Position 4-6)

#### 2. Gamma.app
**Ranking for:** "AI presentation generator," "web-native presentations," "narrative storytelling"
**Gaps to exploit:**
- Weak on professional/consulting use cases
- More focused on storytelling than structured analysis
- No framework library

**Keywords to target:**
- AI slide generator (Position 4-7)
- interactive presentations (Position 3-5)

#### 3. Slideworks.io
**Direct competitor in consulting templates**
**Ranking for:** "McKinsey templates," "consulting slide library," "BCG frameworks"
**Gaps to exploit:**
- Static templates vs. AI generation
- No "instant generation" capability
- Expensive one-time purchases vs. SaaS model

**Keywords to target (and outrank):**
- McKinsey presentation templates (Position 2-4)
- consulting PowerPoint templates (Position 3-5)

#### 4. Plus AI (formerly PlusDocs)
**Ranking for:** "AI for Google Slides," "AI slide generator," "automated presentations"
**Gaps to exploit:**
- Add-on approach vs. standalone platform
- Limited consulting framework expertise
- Generic output quality

#### 5. SlideModel / SlideScience
**Template marketplaces with strong SEO**
**Ranking for:** "consulting templates," "McKinsey slides," "business presentation templates"
**Gaps to exploit:**
- Manual template browsing vs. AI generation
- One-time purchases vs. SaaS value
- No AI assistance
